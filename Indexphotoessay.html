A green haven in the Parisian urban jungle could soon be covered by concrete.

The fight is not over

The pool project is only the first step. 15 additional gardens are also under threat in the medium run, because of another development project. This time, the concreters want to build hotels, office buildings and a subway station, according to Dolores. As concrete eats up the gardens patch by patch, their victory appears inevitable.
